PooPlot

Track, view and share your poops.

What is PooPlot?

PooPlot is a mobile app that allows you to track, view and share your poops. It is quickly showing promise of disrupting the pooping industry.

Explore

Explore where people are pooping all over the world. Like, comment and share any poops.

Social

PooPlot has numerous social functionalities. Including Liking, Commenting and Following your favourite poopers.

Why did you make this?

I made PooPlot to develop my skills as an engineer :). I wanted to create a product that was unique, yet engineered to a high standard.

Umm, Eww?

What is it made in?

PooPlot was developed using Ionic 3. Ionic 3 is basically a hybrid mobile framework that allows you to develop once and deploy everywhere!
